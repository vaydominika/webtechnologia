Attack on Titan - 進撃の巨人

About the Anime

Attack on Titan is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name, created by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from Titans, gigantic humanoid creatures that devour humans. The series explores themes of survival, freedom, and the moral complexities of war, as the characters confront the terrifying reality of their existence.

Main Characters

  • Erin Yeager: The main protagonist of the series, Erin is a determined and passionate young man who dreams of exploring the world beyond the walls. After witnessing the brutal death of his mother at the hands of a Titan, he vows to eradicate all Titans. Erin possesses the unique ability to transform into a Titan himself, which becomes a pivotal aspect of the story as he seeks to uncover the truth behind the Titans and his own powers.
  • Mikasa Ackerman: Mikasa is Erin's adoptive sister and one of the most skilled soldiers in the Survey Corps. She is fiercely protective of Erin and will go to great lengths to keep him safe. Mikasa's exceptional combat skills and unwavering loyalty make her a formidable ally in the fight against the Titans. Her backstory reveals a tragic past that shapes her character and motivations throughout the series.
  • Armin Arlert: Armin is Erin's childhood friend and a strategic thinker. Although he lacks physical strength compared to his peers, his intelligence and resourcefulness often prove invaluable in critical situations. Armin's character development throughout the series showcases his growth from a timid boy into a courageous leader who plays a crucial role in the fight against the Titans.
